Ella Baker

Born December 13th 1903, Ella baker and her family came to be very successful farmers after buying land from the plantation her grandmother was previously enslaved at. Baker then went on to study at Shaw University, where she challenged school policies she thought were unfair. From there, she joined social activist orginizations.In 1930, she joined the Young Negros cooperative League, where she helped develop black economic power. Ella Baker passed away on December 13th, 1986.
